Regular weekly classes with professional tutors

Classes are held both individually and in groups, and the cost will depend on your skills and experience. More favorable conditions are offered to those who study on a regular basis and purchase subscriptions. For those who want to improve their skills, special programs have been developed in various areas (competition, dressage, etc.), including participation in competitions and championships. For the youngest horse fans, pony clubs have been organized, where children can learn to care for animals and ride cute and friendly ponies.

Why choose us?

Convenient location

We are located near Sherwood Forest, offering easy access and connection to nature.

Dining area

Our establishment has a canteen where you can have a quick snack and rest for a while. Tea, coffee and cookies are offered free of charge.

Professional trainers

Our teachers have awards in equestrian sports and excellent feedback from our students.

Free parking

We have a large parking lot so you can leave your car without any problems.

Club directions

Horse riding lessons

During lessons with experienced instructors, you will gain basic riding skills, learn the correct seating position and control techniques.

Horseback riding with a trainer

Horseback riding with a trainer is a great way to enjoy nature and develop your riding confidence.

Photo sessions

During such sessions, a professional photographer will follow your every move, capturing vivid emotions and magnificent shots against the backdrop of picturesque landscapes.

Horse care classes

During the lessons you will learn the basics of care: from proper nutrition and hoof care to daily cleaning and mane styling.

Christmas horse ridings

Christmas horse riding is a tradition that is practiced in many cultures during the Christmas season.

Night horse riding

Under the soft light of the moon and twinkling stars, you can enjoy the atmosphere of peace and beauty as you travel along the picturesque trails.
